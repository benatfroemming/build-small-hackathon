## 💭 What is Emoji Studio?

Emoji Studio is a chat app that explores how people and AI can communicate in new ways. Instead of just using existing tokens, you and the assistant create your own emojis together, decide what they mean, and start using them in your conversations.

### ✨ How Emoji Studio Works

Emoji Studio is a chat app where you can design your own custom emojis and use them in conversation with an AI assistant.

### 🎨 Creating an emoji

Just ask for one in natural language, for example, "make me a happy hippo emoji" or "create an emoji of a steaming pizza slice." The assistant will generate a brand-new image for it automatically.

### 📝 Giving it meaning

Once your emoji is generated, you'll be asked what it means and when you'd use it (e.g. "feeling excited about food" or "use when something is hot"). This helps the assistant know when it's appropriate to use your emoji later. If you are unhappy with the generated emoji or want to abort, just type 'cancel'.

### 😊 Using your emojis

Once saved, your emoji becomes available in two ways:

- Click the emoji picker button next to the message box to browse and insert any of your custom emojis directly into your message.
- The assistant can also use your emojis on its own, weaving them naturally into its replies when it fits the context.

### 📦 Your emoji collection

Every emoji you create is added to your personal collection and stays available throughout your session. Hover over an emoji in the picker to see its name.

### 🧹 Clearing the chat

The Clear button resets the conversation but keeps your custom emoji collection intact, so you don't have to recreate them.
